Scaled Composites

A Redesigned Logo

Scaled Composites is an American aerospace company that focuses on pursuing cost-effective development projects delivered on a short schedule. I decided to redesign their logo to be more scalable and less "engineery." I took into consideration as to what they create and use symbolic representation to portray their company.

From looking into their company, I come to find out that they create unqie aircrafts. Going off of aircrafts, I brainstormed other ideas on flight itself. Not only do airplanes come to mind, but birds. Birds are a type of species that soar through the sky. Bringing in all that information, the redesigned logo is what we see here.

Client: Scaled Composites
