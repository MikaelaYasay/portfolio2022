Dubai Tourism Logo

International Way-finding Signage

I was given the opportunity to create a tourism logo for an International desitnation. For that, I designed a logo for Dubai of the United Arab Emirates. Upon research, Dubai is one of the most wealthiest countries in the world. The reason for its wealth, was the pearl industry. Pearl diving was the foundation and the growth of Dubai, and was the main source of income for many of the locals. Since then, Dubai has grown and built some astounding architecture. I wanted to capture that wealth, beauty, and the history of Dubai and how it became what it is today.

Client: Dubai Tourism
Service: Visual Graphics
Skills: Adobe Illustrator
