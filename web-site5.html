This is Love

A Book Trilogy

This project was to create a book cover trilogy based off of a This is Love podcast by Phoebe Judge. Phoebe Judge had quite a bit of podcasts to choose from but, I decided to take a chance on podcasts "What are we going to do, How to live forever, and Eight thousand miles."

Steps on how I constructed my trilogy:

1. Listening to the podcast. Get to know the story, listen to what the speakers have to say.

2. Listen to the podcast again and take some notes. Write down information that will help you sketch out ideas about the story without giving it away.

3. Sketch out your ideas. Create as many sketches as you need.

4. Out of all the sketches you have, 3 of your best ones will be what you refine.

5. Within the 3, only one will be what you refine more and finalize as your book cover.

6. With your final book cover design, you can create mockups that will display and highlight your work.
