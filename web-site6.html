USPS Stamps

State Floral Edition

We were given the opportunity to work in a collaborative project to design a cohesive set of stamps for the United States Postal Service. Amongst the group, we discussed ideas on a potential theme and ideate ways in which we can express the theme. The group took time after class to brainstorm, and research our theme, and agreed to do state florals. Once we had our theme, we would collectively discuss the typography, the layout and medium of the designs in a way that would be coherent to each others.

Client: Emily Moody
Service: Print | Layout
Skills: Adobe Illustrator | Photoshop | InDesign
